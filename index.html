Conditions of Participation

There are always questions about who can participate in any cause, below are some of the most frequently asked questions answered.

When can I apply for a volunteer opportunity?

You can apply for different roles at any time. However these role are popular and many fill up very quickly, so if you are interested we suggest you express you interest early by contacting, or if you're ready to sign up then book your place. A lot of new projects come to us through our partners so this is a good time to check and book for short term projects.(3months, 6months, 12months

Who can apply ?

Love response offers amazing opportunities for volunteers aged 16-updards in and Outside Uganda who want to volunteer on different projects.

Can we donate clothes?

All forms of help are needed as they contribute in one way or another to the final goal

What is the minimum amount to donate?

Theres not a minimum amount, 1 by 1 makes up a bundle

Can we show sponsors?

Sponsors can choose to either be showed or not

Does this work only in Uganda?

Yes for now, but we understand these problems similarly occur in many places of the world and we are soon looking to extend the helping hand.

Is medical exam free?

Medical examination of all volunteers will be free whenever they are required to carry out one.

where will I stay while am on a project ?

Every project is different. Some projects you'll be staying with local people, in families (which is great for learning a language) or in small community centers, whilst others you'll be part of a larger group of up to 20 volunteers from all over the world staying in youth hostels or campsites

Do you help with travel fees ?

Love response stipulates that all volunteers cover visa costs and must have their own travel and health insurance. As a minimum you should take out travel insurance that will cover at least medical and repatriation costs but it is always worth having baggage cover and travel cancellation cover too.

What routes should one follow to apply?

To apply, use the apply button on the navigation if you’re eligible.

Do I have to pay a tax (donor)?

All taxes on donations will be covered by the donor.
