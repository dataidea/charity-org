FIND CAUSE to support

Below are our causes and reasons for coming out, find a cause you can contribute.

Improve the quality of education

If you want to contribute positively to the future of an African country you can help build schools that fulfil basic requirements, or you can counsel the students on how to build a better future for themselves. 

Provide food and water

The Water Project is an organization that raises funds to drive clean water campaigns. You can also take part in the World Food Program, which has helped over 100 million people in 80 different countries in Africa. 

Volunteering

The most effective way to help people in Africa is by getting directly involved in the root of problems, and helping to solve them one step at a time.

Donate money to charities and NGOs

Another way you can get involved is by donating money to local working charities and NGOs. . You can use a reputed money wiring service, transfer money online or use a mobile app for transferring funds to Africa.

Other ideas?

For any other way you feel is helpful, please use the contact form below to connect to the concerned personnel, we have a great collaborative team that is ready to listen to different bright ideas

Conditions of Participation

There are always questions about who can participate in any cause, below are some of the most frequently asked questions answered.

How old can I be?

How old. As long as you are old enough to understand the problem, you are old enough to solve it.

Can we donate clothes?

Can we? All forms of help are needed as they contribute in one way or another to the final goal

What is the minimum amount to donate?

Theres not a minimum amount, 1 by 1 makes up a bundle

Can we show sponsors?

sponsors can be showed as they are the reason the aid is possible

Does this work only in Africa?

Yes for now, but we understand these problems similarly occur in many places of the world and we are soon looking to extend the helping hand.

Is medical exam free ?

Medical examination of all volunteers will be free whenever they are required to carry out one.

Do you help with passport?

For foreign volunteers, help with passports will be offered whenever it is possible.

What routes should one follow?

Please use the contact form provided below to get in touch about this.

Do I have to pay a tax?

All taxes on donations will be paid by the donor.
